Everyday I was super busy in studying, felt stressful all the time. So, at night occasionally, I would go aimlessly on the street, just to feel the crowds passing by me.
I remember specially one time during the Christmas, the trees on the square were full of small colorful lights, then I just stood there looking at people. ☘ I almost heard the opera sound around. The most enjoyable moment for me is this quietness in the bustle and noise.
